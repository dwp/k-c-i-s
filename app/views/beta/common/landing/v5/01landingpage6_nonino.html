If you are unable to check safety measures or make a report related to a customer

This may be because you have the wrong access to our Keep Customer Interactions Safe (KCIS) service.

To resolve this, ask your manager to email kcis.feedback@dwp.gov.uk with ‘Wrong access’ in the email ‘Subject’. In the email, your manager should:

  • include your email address and
  • confirm that you regularly interact with customers in your job.

While you wait for your access, you can still check safety measures or report an incident or safety concern.

Check safety measures recorded against a customer

Ask your manager to check for you.

If your manager is not able to do this, email kcis.feedback@dwp.gov.uk with ‘URGENT: Safety Measures Required’ in the email ‘Subject’. We will reply as soon as possible.

Report an incident or safety concern

Ask your manager or another colleague to report on your behalf.

Or, go to How do I report an incident on the Keep Customer Interactions Safe Service? and complete the ‘Clerical form to report an unsafe interaction’ at the bottom of the page. Once complete, email this form to the email address on the form.

You should complete this form, even if you do not have the customer’s National Insurance number or know their name.
