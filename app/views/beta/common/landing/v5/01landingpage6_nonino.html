Report an incident or safety concern

Ask your manager or another colleague to report on your behalf.

Or, go to How do I report an incident on the Keep Customer Interactions Safe Service? and complete the ‘Clerical form to report an unsafe interaction’ at the bottom of the page. Once complete, email this form to the email address on the form.

You should complete this form, even if you do not have the customer’s National Insurance number or know their name.
