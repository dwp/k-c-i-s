Do you have the wrong access?

If you are expecting to be able to make a report related to a known customer or to check safety measures then you may have the wrong permissions.

Ask your manager to email kcis.feedback@dwp.gov.uk with a subject of "Wrong access" and include your email address and a confirmation that you routinely interact with customers as part of your day-to-day job.

Preparing for a safe interaction

Ask your manager to check safety measures for you.

If your manager is not able to do this then email kcis.feedback@dwp.gov.uk with a subject of "URGENT: Safety measures required. We will reply as soon as possible.
