Preparing for an interaction

If you a preparing for a customer interaction such as a phone call or meeting, you may need to check if there are measures you need to take. Talk to your manager to understand if they can check for you or approve access.

If you have the wrong access

Ask your manager to email kcis.feedback@dwp.gov.uk with a subject of "Wrong access" explaining why you need access to either online reporting or preparing for interactions using KCIS.
